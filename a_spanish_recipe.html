A SPANISH RECIPE

WRITE A SPANISH RECIPE

Now it is your turn to show how much you know about your country's cooking.

You have to work in PAIRS and write a SPANISH RECIPE using CANVA.

INSTRUCTIONS

1. SEE THE VIDEO TO GET IDEAS FOR YOUR PROJECT.

2. SEARCH THE WEB FOR IDEAS.
You can use GOOGLE for example.

3. WRITE DOWN YOUR IDEAS IN YOUR PAPER.

4. USE CANVA EDUCATION(YOU HAVE INVITED THROUGH CLASSROOM)  TO CREATE A RECIPE FOR A SPANISH DISH.

5. PUBLISH YOUR RECIPE IN CLASSROOM.
