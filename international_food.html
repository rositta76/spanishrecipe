INTERNATIONAL FOOD

Dictionary

Dessert

Definition:

Sweet food eaten at the end of a meal.

Alimentos dulces que se comen al final de una comida.

Example:

For dessert there's apple pie or fruit.

De postre hay tarta de manzana o una pieza de fruta.

Spanish word:

Postre

Audio:

Fish and chips

Definition:

A typical English dish made of fried fish and fried potatoes.

Un plato típico inglés hecho de pescado frito y patatas fritas.

Example:

You can eat fish and chips in London.

Puedes comer pescado con patatas en Londres.

Spanish word:

Pescado con patatas

Audio:

Fizzy drink

Definition:

A cold drink full of gas bubbles with different flavours.

Una bebida fría con burbujas de diferentes sabores.

Example:

I like my fizzy drink very cold.

Me gusta mi refresco muy frío.

Spanish word:

Refresco

Audio:

Fortune cookie

Definition:

A Chinese cookie containing a paper with a proverb or prediction.

Una galleta china que contiene un papel con un proverbio o predicción.

Example:

My sister doesn’t like fortune cookies.

A mi hermana no le gustan las galletas de la fortuna.

Spanish word:

Galleta de la fortuna

Audio:

Hot-dog

Definition:

Sandwich with a sausage.

Bocadillo con una salchicha.

Example:

Can I have a hot dog?

¿Puedo comerme un perrito caliente?

Spanish word:

Perrito caliente

Audio:

Macaroni

Definition:

An Italian food, made of short thick tubes of pasta.

Una comida italiana hecha de tubos cortos de pasta.

Example:

I prefer macaroni and tomato.

Prefiero los macarrones con tomate.

Spanish word:

Macarrones

Audio:

Main

Definition:

The most important.

Principal, el más importante.

Example:

The main dish.

El plato principal.

Spanish word:

Principal (plato)

Audio:

Milkshake

Definition:

A cold drink made of milk, with some flavour, and ice cream sometimes.

Una bebida fría hecha de leche, algún sabor y a veces helado.

Example:

I prefer vanilla milkshake.

Prefiero el batido de vainilla.

Spanish word:

Batido

Audio:

Mince pie

Definition:

A pie filled with meat.

Un pastel relleno de carne.

Example:

We eat mince pie for Christmas.

Nosotros comemos pastel de carne en Navidad.

Spanish word:

Pastel de carne

Audio:

Noodles

Definition:

Like spaghetti but thinner.

Como los spaghettis pero más finos.

Example:

My dad loves noodles.

A mi papá le encantan los fideos.

Spanish word:

Fideos

Audio:

Pancake

Definition:

A flat, round, batter cake fried on both sides.

Un pastel redondo y aplastado de masa frita por ambos lados.

Example:

I prefer pancakes with chocolate and fruit.

Prefiero las tortitas con chocolate y fruta.

Spanish word:

Tortita

Audio:

Spring roll

Definition:

A Chinese pancake that is rolled up with vegetables or meat in it.

Un panqueque chino que se enrolla con verduras o carne.

Example:

We eat spring rolls every Monday.

Comemos rollos de primavera todos los lunes.

Spanish word:

Rollo de primavera

Audio:

Starter

Definition:

Appetizer.

Aperitivo.

Example:

I would like a salad as a starter.

Me gustaría una ensalada como entrante.

Spanish word:

Entrante (plato)

We are learning…

Do you like...?

INTERROGATIVE SENTENCES 

HOT DOGS

When we ask if you like a certain food, we use the plural nouns:

Do you like hot dogs?

TOMATOES

Do you like tomatoes?

FISH

The plural for fish is fish.

Do you like fish?

CAN

We use can to ask for food and to express a request.

Can I have some water, please?

Can I have some rice?

Can I have an orange juice?

CAN



Have (food) for (meal)

You can say what and when you eat this way:

Have (food) for (meal)

For example: I have cereal with milk for breakfast.
